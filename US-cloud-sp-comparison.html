The AI Workload Catalyst

AI isn't just driving compute; it's driving distinct layers of cloud services. Microsoft is capturing the high-level PaaS/SaaS AI API market, whereas Amazon and Google are battling fiercely for the foundational model training and proprietary silicon (IaaS) tier.

Infrastructure (IaaS) AWS Leads

Raw compute, custom silicon (Trainium/Inferentia), and massive GPU clusters for foundation model builders.

Platform APIs (PaaS) Azure Leads

Managed AI services, OpenAI endpoints, and developer tooling for enterprise application integration.

Data & Analytics GCP Strong

Vector databases, BigQuery ML integrations, and open-source model ecosystems (Vertex AI).
